## Getting started

Deposit USDC into a strategy, receive a **Strategy Token** and an **Expected Points Token**. Sell ST on the ArcX AMM, hold EPT for leveraged points exposure, or use the **flash loop** to maximize capital efficiency.

$$
1 \text{ USDC deposited} = \frac{1}{R} \text{ ST} + 1 \text{ EPT}
$$

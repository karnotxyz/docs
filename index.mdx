ArcX tokenizes perp DEX trading strategies. Deposit USDC, receive two tokens: a **Strategy Token** (ST) for USDC returns and an **Expected Points Token** (EPT) for exchange points. Trade them separately or use the **flash loop** to maximize capital efficiency.

$$
1 \text{ USDC deposited} = \frac{1}{R} \text{ ST} + 1 \text{ EPT}
$$

## Choose your path

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Points Farmer">
    Maximize your exchange points exposure with leveraged EPT.

    1. Deposit \$100 USDC into a strategy
    2. Receive 100 EPT + ST tokens
    3. Sell ST on the ArcX AMM at a ~10% discount (\$90)
    4. Flash loop: re-deposit the \$90 for more EPT
    5. Result: ~270 EPT for ~\$27 effective cost

    On perp trading terminals, you trade to earn points but your capital takes trading losses along the way. ArcX separates the points from the PnL risk.

    [Get started](/learn/user-guide) | [How flash loops work](/learn/protocol-overview)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Yield Seeker">
    Earn fixed APR by buying discounted Strategy Tokens from points farmers.

    1. Buy ST at \$0.90 on the ArcX AMM (points farmers dump ST)
    2. Strategy earns ~1% over the epoch
    3. Redeem at ~\$1.01 at maturity
    4. **11% return per epoch = ~44% annualized APR**

    No points exposure, no directional risk on exchange tokens.

    [Get started](/learn/user-guide) | [ST pricing mechanics](/deep-dives/ept-pricing)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What ArcX does

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Splits strategies into two tradeable tokens" icon="scissors">
    Every USDC deposit mints a Strategy Token (tracks USDC returns) and an Expected Points Token (tracks exchange points). Each can be held, sold, or redeemed independently.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Flash loop for leveraged points exposure" icon="bolt">
    Atomically deposit, sell ST, and re-deposit in a single transaction. Get 3-10x more EPT exposure than a simple deposit, without borrowing or liquidation risk.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom AMM with time-decay pricing" icon="chart-line">
    ST trades on a Pendle-style AMM where the discount narrows as maturity approaches. Points farmers sell ST at a discount; yield seekers buy the discount.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cross-chain from any major L1/L2" icon="bridge">
    Deposit from Ethereum, Arbitrum, Solana, and more via LayerZero. Strategies trade across Pacifica, Hyperliquid, Extended, and other perp DEXs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

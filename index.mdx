---
title: "ArcX Documentation"
description: "ArcX splits perp DEX trading strategies into separately tradeable tokens: one for USDC returns, one for exchange points."
---

ArcX tokenizes perp DEX strategies into two outcomes. Deposit USDC into a strategy, receive a **Strategy Token** (your claim on USDC returns) and an **Expected Points Token** (your claim on exchange points). Sell ST on the ArcX AMM, hold EPT for leveraged points exposure, or use the **flash loop** to maximize capital efficiency.

$$
1 \text{ USDC deposited} = \frac{1}{R} \text{ ST} + 1 \text{ EPT}
$$

*Ignoring deposit fees. See [The Three Tokens](/learn/token-economics) for the precise formula.*

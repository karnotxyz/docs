---

ArcX tokenizes perp DEX strategies into two outcomes. Deposit USDC into a strategy vault, receive a **Strategy Token (ST)** — your claim on USDC returns — and an **Expected Points Token (EPT)** — your claim on exchange points. Each token trades on its own orderbook: **EPT/USDC** and **ST/USDC**. Points farmers buy EPT. Yield seekers buy discounted ST. Each user gets exactly the exposure they want, at a price set by the market.

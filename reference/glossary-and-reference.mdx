## Glossary

### A

**Admin**
Our admin address with administrative control over strategy epochs and parameters. *Example: Admin finalizes an epoch after the strategy unwind is complete, enabling redemptions.*

### C

**Credit**
The unit that determines your share of points. Credits accumulate per-EPT from periodic credit updates. More EPT held, for longer, during higher-activity updates = more credits. *Example: Holding 100 EPT across 50 updates, each at 20 credits/EPT, produces 100,000 credits.*

**Credits Oracle**
Our off-chain service that periodically pushes credit updates to the EPT contract during the epoch. Each update reports a credits-per-EPT value based on strategy activity. *Example: Strategy activity increases, so the next update reports a higher credits-per-EPT value.*

**Cross-chain deposit**
A deposit originating from a non-Starknet chain (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Solana). USDC is locked on the source chain, a cross-chain message delivers the deposit instruction to Starknet, and tokens are minted at arrival-time NAV. If the message never arrives, you can cancel after a timeout and get your USDC back.

**Cross-match**
When a buy-EPT order and a buy-ST order at compatible prices are matched together, the engine mints new ST + EPT from deposited USDC and fills both sides. This is how new tokens enter circulation via the orderbooks. *Example: Alice bids \$0.10 for EPT, Bob bids \$0.90 for ST. The engine deposits \$1, mints both tokens, fills both orders.*

### D

**Deposit cutoff**
How long before the epoch ends that deposits stop being accepted. Configurable per strategy. *Example: Deposits stop 1 hour before the epoch ends.*

**Deposit fee**
A fee on USDC deposits that covers operational costs. Configurable per strategy. The fee is deducted before shares are calculated.

### E

**Epoch**
A fixed-duration window (typically 8-12 weeks) during which a strategy runs. Each epoch creates new ST and EPT instances. Epochs progress through three states: ACTIVE, MATURITY, FINALIZED.

**EPT (Expected Points Token)**
Your claim on exchange points earned during the epoch, weighted by holding duration and strategy activity. One EPT per epoch per exchange per strategy. Tradeable on the EPT/USDC orderbook. *Example: Alice buys 100 EPT on the orderbook and holds them through the epoch, accruing credits that convert to points at finalization.*

**EPT implied cost**
The theoretical cost of EPT derived from minting parity: EPT = 1 - X/R, where X = ST market price and R = NAV. *Example: ST trades at \$0.90, NAV = \$1.00. Implied EPT cost = 1 - 0.90 = \$0.10.*

### F

**Finalization**
The event where we publish the final NAV and total points for an epoch. After finalization, ST redeems for USDC and EPT claims PointsTokens. *Example: Epoch 7 ends, positions unwind over 1-2 days, admin finalizes. Users can now redeem.*

**Final Points Oracle**
Our off-chain service that reports the total points earned by the strategy during the epoch. Called once, after maturity. *Example: The strategy earned 12,500 points during Epoch 7. The oracle reports this number to the contract.*

### M

**Minting parity**
The conservation law for token creation: 1 USDC deposited = (1/R) ST + 1 EPT. The value of the parts equals the value of the whole. This relationship also governs cross-matching on the orderbooks. *Example: If NAV = \$1.00, depositing \$1 produces 1 ST share + 1 EPT.*

**Multi-EPT**
When a strategy earns points on multiple exchanges, a single deposit produces separate EPT tokens for each exchange. *Example: Depositing into a cross-exchange strategy produces 1 ST + EPT for exchange A + EPT for exchange B.*

### N

**NAV (Net Asset Value)**
The per-share USDC value of the strategy vault: total vault value divided by total shares outstanding. *Example: Vault holds \$101,500, 100,000 shares outstanding. NAV = \$1.015.*

**NAV Oracle**
Our off-chain service that periodically reports strategy NAV. Computed from exchange position data (principal + unrealized PnL + fees).

### O

**Orderbook**
A limit order book where users place buy and sell orders for EPT/USDC or ST/USDC. Orders are matched off-chain and settled on-chain on Starknet. *Example: Alice places a limit buy for 100 EPT at \$0.08. When a matching sell order appears, the trade executes and settles on-chain.*

### P

**Pause**
Admin action that stops new deposits into an epoch. Does not affect orderbook trading, credit accrual, or redemptions.

**PointsToken**
On-chain representation of exchange points, 1:1 backed by real points in our exchange account. One PointsToken per exchange. Persists across epochs. *Example: Alice claims 450 PointsTokens from Epoch 7. She already had 320 from earlier epochs. Her total: 770.*

### R

**Redemption fee**
Fee deducted when claiming PointsTokens from EPT credits. Configurable per strategy.

**Refund timeout**
If a cross-chain deposit is not acknowledged within this window, you can cancel and recover your USDC on the source chain.

### S

**Secondary trade**
A trade between existing token holders on the orderbook. No new tokens are minted or burned; ownership transfers between buyer and seller. *Example: Alice sells 50 EPT to Bob on the EPT/USDC orderbook.*

**ST (Strategy Token)**
Your claim on the strategy's USDC value at finalization. One ST per epoch per strategy. Tradeable on the ST/USDC orderbook during the epoch, redeemable for USDC after finalization. *Example: Alice deposits \$100, receives ST shares. The strategy makes 2%. She redeems for approximately \$102.*

**Strategy**
An actively managed trading approach executed by us on supported perp DEXes. Each strategy has its own vault, producing unique ST and EPT tokens each epoch. *Example: A funding arb strategy goes long on one exchange and short on another to capture funding rate differentials.*

### T

**TGE (Token Generation Event)**
When a perp DEX launches its token and distributes airdrops. After TGE, PointsTokens become redeemable for airdrop tokens. *Example: A perp DEX conducts TGE. PointsToken holders burn their tokens to receive airdrop tokens.*

**Credit Update**
A single oracle update, reporting the credits-per-EPT accrued since the last update. Each update reflects the strategy's activity level during that interval. *Example: An update reports 25 credits/EPT. All EPT holders accrue 25 credits per EPT they hold.*

## Token Reference

| Token | Scope | Tradeable | Redeemable for | Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ST** | Per epoch, per strategy | Yes (ST/USDC orderbook) | USDC (after finalization) | End of epoch |
| **EPT** | Per epoch, per exchange, per strategy | Yes (EPT/USDC orderbook) | PointsTokens (after finalization) | End of epoch |
| **PointsToken** | Per exchange (persists across epochs) | Yes | Airdrop tokens (after TGE) | None |

### Naming Convention

| Token | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| ST | ST-{Strategy}-E{epoch} | ST-FundingArb-E007 |
| EPT | EPT-{Exchange}-E{epoch} | EPT-Paradex-E007 |
| PointsToken | x{Exchange} | xPX, xHL, xPC |

## Key Equations

| Equation | What it means | Where it's used |
|---|---|---|
| 1 USDC = (1/R) ST + 1 EPT | One dollar deposited produces this fixed combination of tokens | Deposit flow, cross-matching |
| NAV = vault value / total shares | Each ST share is worth the vault value divided by total shares | ST pricing, redemption |
| shares = deposit amount / NAV | Shares minted depend on current per-share NAV | Deposit calculation |
| your points = (your credits / total credits) x total points | Your fraction of credits equals your fraction of points | Points distribution |
| EPT implied cost = 1 - X/R | The gap between \$1 and the value of ST from a \$1 deposit | EPT pricing |

## Epoch State Machine

| State | Deposits | Credit Accrual | Orderbook Trading | Redemption |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ACTIVE** | Yes | Yes (credit updates) | Yes | No |
| **MATURITY** | No | Stopped | Yes | No |
| **FINALIZED** | No | Final credits settled | No | Yes |

## Trust Surface

| Component | Trust Model | Verifiable? |
|---|---|---|
| NAV Oracle | Operated by us | Partially (exchange dashboards) |
| Credits Oracle | Operated by us | Partially (observable activity) |
| Final Points Oracle | Operated by us | Partially (exchange programs) |
| PointsToken backing | Reported by us | Not currently |
| Strategy execution | Operated by us | Not on-chain |
| Orderbook matching | Operated by us | Settlement is on-chain |
| Cross-chain deposits | Multisig + refund timeout | Refund timeout as safety valve |
| Smart contracts | On-chain | Fully (code is on-chain) |

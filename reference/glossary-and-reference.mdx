## Glossary

### A

**Admin**
The ArcX-operated address with privileged access to protocol functions (pause, finalize, configure fees). *Example: Admin finalizes an epoch after the strategy unwind is complete, enabling ST and EPT redemptions.*

### C

**Credit**
The holding measure that determines a user's share of points, accumulated per-EPT from periodic oracle ticks. More EPT held, for longer, during higher-activity ticks = more credits. *Example: Holding 100 EPT across 50 oracle ticks, each reporting 20 credits/EPT, produces 100,000 credits.*

**Credits Oracle**
ArcX-operated off-chain service that periodically pushes credit ticks to EPT contracts during the epoch. Each tick reports the credits-per-EPT accrued since the previous tick. *Example: The Credits Oracle observes that strategy activity increased and pushes a higher credits-per-EPT value in the next tick.*

**Cross-chain deposit**
A deposit originating from a non-Starknet chain (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Solana). USDC is locked in a SourceChainDepositEscrow, a LayerZero message delivers the deposit instruction to Starknet, and tokens are minted at arrival-time NAV. *Example: Alice deposits from Ethereum. Her USDC is locked for a few minutes while LayerZero delivers the message. ST + EPT are minted on Starknet when the message arrives.*

**Cross-match**
The process where a buy-EPT order and a buy-ST order at compatible prices are matched by the engine, which mints new ST + EPT tokens from deposited USDC to fill both sides. This is how new tokens enter circulation via the orderbooks. *Example: Alice places a buy-EPT order at \$0.10 and Bob places a buy-ST order at \$0.90. The engine deposits \$1, mints 1 ST + 1 EPT, and fills both orders.*

### D

**Deposit cutoff**
Configurable parameter (`depositCutoff`) specifying how many seconds before `epochEnd` deposits stop being accepted. *Example: With `depositCutoff = 3600`, deposits stop 1 hour before the epoch ends.*

**Deposit fee**
A fee charged on gross USDC at deposit time. Covers operational costs. Configurable per strategy. *Example: A deposit fee is applied to a \$100 deposit, and the net USDC is used to mint ST shares and EPT.*

### E

**Epoch**
A fixed-duration window during which a strategy runs. Each epoch creates new ST and EPT contract instances. Epochs progress through three states: ACTIVE, MATURITY, FINALIZED. *Example: Epoch 7 runs for several weeks. Epoch 8 starts after Epoch 7 finalizes.*

**epochId**
Unique identifier for an epoch. Encoded in contract deployments and token names. *Example: `ST-FundingArb-E007` is the ST token for Epoch 7 of the Funding Arb strategy.*

**EPT (Expected Points Token)**
ERC20 with built-in credit accrual. Represents a claim on exchange points earned during the epoch, weighted by holding duration and strategy activity. One EPT contract per epoch per exchange per strategy. Tradeable on the EPT/USDC orderbook. *Example: Alice buys 100 EPT on the EPT/USDC orderbook. She holds them through the epoch, accruing credits that convert to points at finalization.*

**EPT implied cost**
The theoretical cost of EPT derived from the minting parity equation: `EPT_cost = 1 - X/R`, where X = ST market price on the orderbook and R = ST exchange rate. *Example: ST trades at \$0.90, exchange rate R = 1.0. EPT_cost = 1 - 0.90/1.0 = \$0.10.*

### F

**Finalization**
The event where the admin finalizes the epoch after the NAV Oracle reports finalNAV and the Final Points Oracle reports totalPoints. Enables ST to USDC redemption and EPT to PointsToken claiming. *Example: Epoch 7 ends. Within a few days, positions are unwound and USDC is returned. Admin finalizes. Users can now redeem.*

**Final Points Oracle**
ArcX-operated off-chain service that reports the total number of exchange points earned during the epoch. Called once per epoch, after maturity. *Example: The strategy earned 12,500 points during Epoch 7. The Final Points Oracle reports totalPoints to the EPT contract.*

### L

**LayerZero (LZ)**
The cross-chain messaging protocol used to deliver deposit instructions from source chains to Starknet. *Example: Alice's deposit message travels from Ethereum to Starknet via LayerZero.*

### M

**MATURITY**
The second epoch state. Entered when the epoch end time is reached. Strategy positions are unwound, no new deposits accepted, and settlement begins. *Example: Epoch 7 enters MATURITY when the epoch end timestamp is reached. The strategy runner closes positions on supported perp DEXes.*

**Minting parity**
The protocol-enforced relationship: `1 USDC deposited = (1/R) ST + 1 EPT`. This equation defines the conservation law for token creation and is the basis for cross-matching on the orderbooks. *Example: If R = 1.0, depositing \$1 produces 1 ST share + 1 EPT. The cross-matching engine uses this ratio when filling compatible buy-EPT and buy-ST orders.*

**Multi-EPT**
The pattern where a single deposit into a cross-exchange strategy produces separate EPT tokens for each exchange. *Example: Depositing into a cross-exchange strategy produces 1 ST + EPT for exchange A + EPT for exchange B. Three tokens from one deposit.*

### N

**NAV (Net Asset Value)**
The per-share USDC value of the strategy vault, computed as `vault_value / total_shares`. Determines ST share price. *Example: The vault holds \$101,500 in total value and has 100,000 shares outstanding. NAV = \$1.015 per share.*

**NAV Oracle**
ArcX-operated off-chain service that periodically reports strategy NAV to the ST Vault. Computes NAV from exchange position data (principal + unrealized PnL + fees). *Example: The NAV Oracle reads position values from supported perp DEXes, computes the per-share value, and pushes it to the ST Vault contract.*

### O

**Orderbook**
A limit order book where users place buy and sell orders for EPT/USDC or ST/USDC. Orders are matched off-chain and settled on-chain on Starknet. Each token pair has its own orderbook. *Example: Alice places a limit buy order for 100 EPT at \$0.08 on the EPT/USDC orderbook. When a matching sell order appears, the trade executes.*

### P

**Pause**
Admin action that stops new deposits into an epoch. Does not affect the epoch lifecycle, orderbook trading, credit accrual, or redemptions. *Example: A suspected oracle issue triggers a pause. Existing positions are unaffected, but no new deposits are accepted until the admin unpauses.*

**pointsPerCredit**
The conversion ratio computed at finalization: `totalPoints / totalCredits`. Determines how many points each credit is worth. *Example: totalPoints = 1,000, totalCredits = 864,000,000. pointsPerCredit = 0.000001157. A user with 561,600,000 credits receives 650 points.*

**PointsToken**
ERC20 representing tokenized exchange points, 1:1 backed by real points in ArcX's exchange account. One contract per exchange. Persists across epochs. *Example: Alice claims 450 PointsTokens from Epoch 7. She already had 320 from previous epochs. Her total: 770.*

### R

**Redemption fee**
Fee charged when converting EPT credits to PointsTokens. Deducted from gross PointsTokens before minting. Configurable per EPT contract. *Example: Alice's gross claim is 714 PointsTokens. The redemption fee is deducted, and she receives the net amount.*

**Refund timeout**
Configurable parameter on SourceChainDepositEscrow. If a cross-chain deposit is not acknowledged within this window, the user can cancel to recover locked USDC. *Example: Alice's cross-chain deposit has not been acknowledged within the timeout window. She cancels and gets her USDC back.*

### S

**Secondary trade**
A trade between existing token holders on the EPT/USDC or ST/USDC orderbook. No new tokens are minted or burned; ownership simply transfers between buyer and seller. *Example: Alice sells 50 EPT to Bob on the EPT/USDC orderbook. No minting occurs. Alice's balance decreases, Bob's increases.*

**sharePrice**
The current USDC value of one ST share: `vault_value / totalShares`. Updated every time the NAV Oracle pushes a new value. *Example: Vault value = \$103,000, totalShares = 100,000. sharePrice = \$1.03.*

**shares**
The number of ST tokens a depositor receives, computed as `deposit_amount / NAV`. *Example: Alice deposits \$100 when NAV = \$1.02. She receives 100 / 1.02 = ~98.04 ST shares.*

**SourceChainDepositEscrow**
Contract deployed on each supported source chain. Locks user USDC, sends a deposit message via LayerZero to Starknet, and either releases USDC to the ArcX multisig (on acknowledgment) or refunds to the user (on timeout). *Example: On Ethereum, Alice locks USDC in the escrow contract. Her USDC is held until Starknet acknowledges the deposit.*

**ST (Strategy Token)**
ERC4626-like vault share representing a claim on the epoch's USDC NAV at finalization. One ST contract per epoch per strategy. Tradeable on the ST/USDC orderbook during the epoch, redeemable for USDC after finalization. *Example: Alice deposits \$100 and receives ST shares. The strategy makes 2%. She redeems for approximately \$102.*

**Strategy**
An actively managed trading approach executed by ArcX on supported perp DEXes. Each strategy has its own vault, producing unique ST and EPT tokens each epoch. *Example: A funding arb strategy goes long on one exchange and short on another to capture funding rate differentials.*

**Strategy Runner**
ArcX's off-chain service that executes perp DEX trades using deposited USDC as margin. Operates on exchange accounts controlled by ArcX. *Example: The Strategy Runner opens matching long and short positions across supported perp DEXes to execute the funding arb strategy.*

### T

**TGE (Token Generation Event)**
The moment when a perp DEX launches its token and distributes airdrops. After TGE, PointsTokens become redeemable for airdrop tokens via the Redemption Module. *Example: A perp DEX conducts TGE and distributes tokens. ArcX deposits them into the Redemption Module. PointsToken holders can now burn PointsTokens to receive airdrop tokens.*

**Tick**
A single oracle credit update, periodically reporting the credits-per-EPT accrued since the last update. Each tick reflects the strategy's activity level during that interval. *Example: The Credits Oracle pushes a tick reporting 25 credits/EPT. All EPT holders accrue 25 credits per EPT they hold.*

**totalCredits**
Running sum of all users' settled credits across the epoch. Used as the denominator in points distribution: `yourShare = yourCredits / totalCredits`. *Example: At finalization, totalCredits = 864,000,000. Alice has 561,600,000 credits, giving her a 65% share.*

**totalPoints**
The actual number of exchange points earned during the epoch, reported by the Final Points Oracle at finalization. Used as the numerator in points distribution. *Example: The strategy earned 1,000 points during Epoch 7. totalPoints = 1,000.*

### V

**Vault value**
The total USDC held in the strategy vault, including principal, unrealized PnL, and accrued fees across all positions. Distinct from NAV, which is the per-share value (vault_value / total_shares). *Example: The vault holds \$500,000 across positions on multiple exchanges. With 490,000 shares outstanding, NAV = \$500,000 / 490,000 = \$1.0204 per share.*

## Protocol Parameters

### Configurable (Per Strategy / Per Epoch)

| Parameter | Set by | Contract | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| `depositFeeRate` | Admin | ST Vault | Fee on gross USDC deposits. Varies by strategy. |
| `redeemFeeRate` | Admin | EPT | Fee on PointsToken claims. Varies by strategy. |
| `depositCutoff` | Admin | ST Vault | How long before epochEnd to stop deposits. 0 = accept until maturity. |
| `epochStart` | Deployment | ST Vault | When the epoch begins |
| `epochEnd` | Deployment | ST Vault | When the epoch ends |
| `refundTimeout` | Admin | SourceChainDepositEscrow | Time before unacknowledged cross-chain deposits can be refunded |

### Operational (Off-Chain)

| Parameter | Service | Description |
|---|---|---|
| NAV update frequency | NAV Oracle | How often NAV is pushed on-chain (periodically) |
| Credit tick frequency | Credits Oracle | How often credit ticks are pushed (periodically) |
| Strategy unwind time | Strategy Runner | Time to close positions after epoch end |

## Token Reference

| Token | Type | Scope | Tradeable | Redeemable for | Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ST** | ERC4626-like shares | Per epoch, per strategy | Yes (ST/USDC orderbook) | USDC (after finalization) | End of epoch |
| **EPT** | ERC20 + credits | Per epoch, per exchange, per strategy | Yes (EPT/USDC orderbook) | PointsTokens (after finalization) | End of epoch |
| **PointsToken** | ERC20 | Per exchange (persists across epochs) | Yes (standard ERC20) | Airdrop tokens (after TGE) | None |

### Naming Convention

| Token | Pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| ST | `ST-{Strategy}-E{epochId}` | `ST-FundingArb-E007` |
| EPT | `EPT-{Exchange}-E{epochId}` | `EPT-Exchange-E007` |
| PointsToken | `x{Exchange}` | `xPC`, `xHL` |

## Key Equations

| Equation | Plain English | Used in |
|---|---|---|
| `1 USDC = (1/R) ST + 1 EPT` | One dollar deposited produces this fixed combination of tokens | Protocol overview, deposit flow, cross-matching |
| `sharePrice = vault_value / totalShares` | Each ST share is worth the vault value divided by total shares | ST pricing, redemption |
| `shares = deposit_amount / NAV` | Shares minted depend on current per-share NAV | Deposit calculation |
| `usdcOut = shares x finalNAV / totalShares` | Redemption gives you your proportional slice of final vault value | ST redemption |
| `credits = sum of (tick_value x balance) across all ticks` | Credits accumulate from periodic oracle ticks, weighted by EPT balance | Credit accrual |
| `pointsPerCredit = totalPoints / totalCredits` | Your share of points equals your share of total credits | Points distribution |
| `EPT_cost = 1 - X/R` | EPT implied cost is the gap between \$1 and the market value of ST from a \$1 deposit | EPT pricing |

## Epoch State Machine Quick Reference

| State | Deposits | NAV Updates | Credit Accrual | Orderbook Trading | Redemption |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ACTIVE** | Yes | Periodic | Yes (oracle ticks) | Yes | No |
| **MATURITY** | No | Stopped | Stopped | Yes | No |
| **FINALIZED** | No | Final NAV set | Final credits settled | Yes* | Yes |

*Trading can continue on the orderbooks after finalization, but there is no economic reason to. You can redeem directly.

## Trust Surface Quick Reference

| Component | Trust Model | What's Trusted | Verifiable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| NAV Oracle | Centralized (ArcX) | Accurate NAV reporting | Partially (exchange dashboards) |
| Credits Oracle | Centralized (ArcX) | Fair credit tick reporting | Partially (observable activity) |
| Final Points Oracle | Centralized (ArcX) | Accurate totalPoints | Partially (exchange dashboards) |
| PointsToken backing | Trust-based | 1:1 backing with real points | Not currently |
| Strategy execution | Centralized (ArcX) | Competent, non-malicious trading | Not on-chain |
| Orderbook matching | Centralized (ArcX) | Fair order matching, on-chain settlement | Settlement is on-chain |
| Cross-chain escrow | Multisig + timeout | Multisig honesty | Refund timeout as safety valve |
| Smart contracts | On-chain (audited) | Correct logic | Fully (code is on-chain) |

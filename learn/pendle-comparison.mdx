## Quick Mapping

| Pendle | ArcX | Key Difference |
|---|---|---|
| **PT** (Principal Token) | **ST** (Strategy Token) | Both represent a claim on principal. ST also includes strategy PnL, so redemption value depends on performance. |
| **YT** (Yield Token) | **EPT** (Expected Points Token) | Both accrue value over the epoch and decay to $0 at maturity/finalization. YT accrues yield, EPT accrues credits for a points claim. |
| **SY** (Standardized Yield) | **ST** (Strategy Token) | Pendle wraps on-chain yield-bearing assets into SY. We wrap off-chain perp DEX trading strategies. In both cases, the "principal token" is the wrapper. |
| Pendle AMM (PT <> SY) + orderbook | EPT/USDC + ST/USDC Orderbooks | Pendle has one AMM underlying PT <> SY. We have two separate limit order books with cross-matching. |
| Two-way arbitrage (mint + redeem) | One-way arbitrage (mint only) | Pendle has tighter pricing corridors because both directions are enforceable. We have no early redemption. |

---

## The Big Difference: Market Structure

Pendle routes everything through a single AMM between PT and SY. ArcX runs two separate orderbooks (EPT/USDC and ST/USDC) connected by a cross-matching engine that mints new tokens when buy-side prices align.

Both systems split deposits into a principal claim and a yield/points claim. Both have the same minting parity. Pendle: PT + YT = Underlying. ArcX: (1/R) ST + 1 EPT = $1 deposited.

The other key difference: Pendle wraps on-chain yield-bearing assets. We run our own off-chain perp DEX trading strategies. Pendle doesn't execute strategies — it tokenizes existing yield sources. We execute the strategies ourselves and tokenize the outputs.

## When to Use Which

| If you want to... | Pendle | ArcX |
|---|---|---|
| Earn fixed yield on a yield-bearing asset | Buy PT at a discount | Buy ST at a discount |
| Get leveraged yield/points exposure | Buy YT | Buy EPT on the orderbook |
| Get leveraged pre-TGE points exposure | Indirect (YT on point-earning assets) | Buy EPT on the orderbook |
| Separate strategy PnL from points | Not possible | Buy ST or EPT independently |

Pendle is built for on-chain yield optimization. We are built for perp DEX strategy outcomes: PnL and pre-TGE points. You could reasonably use both.

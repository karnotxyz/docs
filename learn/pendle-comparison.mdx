## Quick Mapping

| Pendle | ArcX | Key Difference |
|---|---|---|
| **PT** (Principal Token) | **ST** (Strategy Token) | Both represent a claim on principal. ST also includes strategy PnL, so redemption value depends on performance. |
| **YT** (Yield Token) | **EPT** (Expected Points Token) | YT streams yield continuously. EPT accrues credits for a points claim at epoch finalization. |
| **SY** (Standardized Yield) | Strategy Vault | Pendle wraps on-chain yield-bearing assets. We wrap off-chain perp DEX trading strategies. |
| Pendle AMM (logit curve) | EPT/USDC + ST/USDC Orderbooks | AMM with time-decay curve vs two separate limit order books with cross-matching. |
| Flash swap (leveraged YT) | N/A | Pendle has atomic flash swaps for leveraged exposure. We have standard orderbook buying. |
| YT decays to \$0 at maturity | EPT converts to PointsTokens at finalization | YT has zero terminal value. EPT retains value tied to eventual TGE. |
| Yield streaming | Credit accrual | YT holders receive SY in real-time. EPT holders accumulate credits, claim PointsTokens after finalization. |
| Two-way arbitrage (mint + redeem) | One-way arbitrage (mint only) | Pendle has tighter pricing corridors because both directions are enforceable. We have no early redemption. |

---

## The Big Difference: Terminal Value

Pendle YT goes to \$0 at maturity — all yield has already been streamed. ArcX EPT converts to PointsTokens at finalization, which represent a claim on actual exchange points with TGE-dependent upside. EPT does not decay to zero.

Everything else — token splitting, minting parity, two-sided markets — is structurally the same. Pendle: PT + YT = Underlying. ArcX: (1/R) ST + 1 EPT = \$1 deposited.

## When to Use Which

| If you want to... | Pendle | ArcX |
|---|---|---|
| Earn fixed yield on a yield-bearing asset | Buy PT at a discount | Not the primary use case |
| Get leveraged yield exposure | Buy YT | Not applicable |
| Get leveraged pre-TGE points exposure | Indirect (YT on point-earning assets) | Buy EPT on the orderbook |
| Separate strategy PnL from points | Not possible | Deposit, sell ST or EPT |
| Buy discounted strategy exposure | Buy PT for fixed yield | Buy ST below NAV |
| Use a fully on-chain system | Pendle | We wrap off-chain strategies |

Pendle is built for on-chain yield optimization. We are built for perp DEX strategy outcomes: PnL and pre-TGE points. You could reasonably use both.

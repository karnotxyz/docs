## What Is a PointsToken

A PointsToken is a 1:1 on-chain representation of an exchange's loyalty points. Each supported perp DEX has its own PointsToken:

| PointsToken | Exchange | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| xPX | Paradex | 1 xPX = 1 Paradex point |
| xHL | Hyperliquid | 1 xHL = 1 Hyperliquid point |
| xPC | Pacifica | 1 xPC = 1 Pacifica point |

Additional PointsTokens are created as new exchanges are integrated. One PointsToken per exchange, shared across all strategies and all epochs on that exchange.

## How Claiming Works

After an epoch finalizes, <Tooltip tip="Expected Points Token. Your claim on exchange points earned during the epoch." href="/learn/expected-points-token">EPT</Tooltip> holders claim their share of the epoch's points as PointsTokens. The number you receive is determined by your credit share:

$$\text{your\_PointsTokens} = \frac{\text{your\_credits}}{\text{total\_credits}} \times \text{total\_points}$$

A small fee (varies by strategy) is deducted before minting.

The claim process:

1. Epoch finalizes, total points for the strategy are reported
2. You claim on your EPT position
3. Your credit share is calculated, fee deducted, PointsTokens minted to your wallet

---

## PointsTokens Persist Across Epochs

Unlike ST and EPT, which are created fresh each epoch, PointsTokens accumulate. Your xPC from Epoch 5 and your xPC from Epoch 8 are the **same token**. They stack in your wallet.

This means your total PointsToken balance reflects your cumulative points exposure across every epoch you have participated in.

PointsTokens are standard tokens on Starknet. You can hold, transfer, or trade them freely.

## Post-TGE: Burn for Airdrop Tokens

When an exchange conducts its <Tooltip tip="Token Generation Event. When a perp DEX launches its token and distributes airdrops to points holders.">TGE</Tooltip> (token generation event):

1. The exchange distributes airdrop tokens to ArcX
2. ArcX makes them available for redemption
3. A conversion rate is set based on total airdrop tokens and total PointsToken supply
4. You burn your PointsTokens to receive airdrop tokens at that rate

**Example:** You hold 1,000 xPC. The exchange airdrops 50,000 PDX tokens against a total xPC supply of 100,000.

$$\text{conversion rate} = \frac{50{,}000 \text{ PDX}}{100{,}000 \text{ xPC}} = 0.5$$

You burn 1,000 xPC and receive 500 PDX.

Airdrops may arrive in multiple batches. You do not have to redeem all at once. Burn a portion now, hold the rest for future batches when the conversion rate may be updated.

## What Drives PointsToken Value

| Factor | Effect |
|---|---|
| TGE timing | Closer TGE reduces time discounting, increasing value |
| Expected airdrop size | More tokens per point increases value |
| Exchange token price (post-TGE) | Direct link via conversion rate |
| Supply growth | New epochs add supply, which can dilute per-token value |

Before TGE, PointsToken value is speculative. It reflects the market's expectation of future airdrop generosity and the exchange token's eventual price. After TGE, value is directly tied to the conversion rate and the exchange token's market price.

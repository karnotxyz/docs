ArcX has two orderbooks, **EPT/USDC** and **ST/USDC**, one for each token. Everything starts with placing a buy order on the right book for your goal.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Points Farmer">
    You want leveraged exposure to exchange points.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the EPT/USDC orderbook">
        Navigate to the EPT/USDC orderbook for the strategy you want (e.g., Bluechip Funding Arb). Each strategy has its own EPT.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Place a buy order for EPT">
        Set your price and quantity. For example, buy 2,000 EPT at \$0.05 each for \$100 total. The lower the EPT price, the more leverage you get on points exposure.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Order is matched">
        Your order fills through one of two paths:
        - **Secondary trade**: matched against someone selling their existing EPT
        - **Cross-match mint**: matched with a simultaneous ST buyer, and the protocol mints both tokens from the combined USDC

        Either way, EPT lands in your wallet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Hold EPT during the epoch">
        Credits accrue automatically as the oracle reports ticks. You do not need to take any action. Credits start fresh from the moment you acquire the EPT.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Claim PointsTokens after finalization">
        Once the epoch is finalized, go to the claims page and claim your PointsTokens. Your share is proportional to your credits:

        $$\text{your points} = \frac{\text{your credits}}{\text{total credits}} \times \text{total points}$$
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Yield Seeker">
    You want predictable USDC returns with no points exposure.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the ST/USDC orderbook">
        Navigate to the ST/USDC orderbook for the strategy you want. Check the current NAV on the strategy dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Place a buy order for ST at a discount">
        Buy ST below the current NAV. For example, if NAV is \$1.00, place a buy at \$0.90, a 10% discount. Your \$100 buys about 111 ST.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Order is matched">
        Your order fills through one of two paths:
        - **Secondary trade**: matched against someone selling their existing ST
        - **Cross-match mint**: matched with a simultaneous EPT buyer, and the protocol mints both tokens from the combined USDC

        ST lands in your wallet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Hold ST during the epoch">
        Your ST tracks the strategy's NAV. If the strategy earns 1% over the epoch, NAV rises to approximately \$1.01. Monitor performance on the ArcX dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Redeem ST after finalization">
        Once the epoch is finalized, go to the redemption page and redeem your ST for USDC at the final NAV.

        $$\text{Return} = \frac{1.01 - 0.90}{0.90} \approx 12.2\%\text{ per epoch}$$
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Exiting Early

You do not have to wait for finalization to exit. Sell your tokens on the orderbook at any time during the active epoch.

- **Sell EPT** on the EPT/USDC orderbook to another buyer at market price
- **Sell ST** on the ST/USDC orderbook to another buyer at market price

This is a secondary trade, not a redemption. Redemption (ST for USDC at NAV, EPT for PointsTokens) only happens after the epoch is finalized. Early exits are at whatever price the market offers.

---

<Info>
Most users buy the specific token they want on the orderbook. Direct minting is for users who want both ST and EPT simultaneously.
</Info>

## Direct Minting

Instead of buying on the orderbook, you can deposit USDC directly into a strategy. You receive both tokens:

$$1 \text{ USDC} = \frac{1}{R} \text{ ST} + 1 \text{ EPT}$$

where $R$ is the current NAV per share.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the strategy and epoch">
    Go to the deposit page and choose the strategy you want to enter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your USDC amount">
    Enter how much USDC you want to deposit (e.g., \$100).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Review the breakdown (ST shares and EPT quantity) and confirm in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive both tokens">
    ST and EPT appear in your wallet in the same transaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Direct minting is useful when orderbook spreads are wide, or when you want exposure to both tokens simultaneously. Most users will prefer buying the specific token they want on the orderbook. See [Protocol Overview](/learn/protocol-overview) for details on the minting equation.

## Cross-Chain Deposits

You can deposit USDC from Ethereum, Arbitrum, Solana, and other supported chains without bridging manually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your source chain wallet">
    Connect your EVM or Solana wallet on the ArcX cross-chain deposit page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Starknet address and deposit amount">
    Specify the Starknet wallet that should receive the tokens, and enter your USDC amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Your USDC is locked in an escrow contract on the source chain. A message is sent to Starknet to complete the deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tokens arrive on Starknet">
    Within 1-5 minutes (typical), your tokens are minted on Starknet and delivered to your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Cross-chain deposits typically complete in 1-5 minutes. If the message never arrives, you can cancel after the timeout period and get your USDC refunded on the source chain.
</Tip>

## After Finalization

When an epoch reaches the **FINALIZED** state, final NAV and total points are locked in. There is no deadline to redeem or claim. Do it whenever you are ready.

### ST Holders: Redeem for USDC

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the redemption page">
    Select the finalized epoch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount of ST to redeem">
    You can redeem all or part of your ST balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and receive USDC">
    Your ST is burned and you receive USDC at the final NAV.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### EPT Holders: Claim PointsTokens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the claims page">
    Select the finalized epoch and the EPT you want to claim.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and receive PointsTokens">
    Your credits are settled, PointsTokens are minted to your wallet based on your credit share.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What to Do with PointsTokens

PointsTokens represent actual exchange points. Hold them until the exchange conducts its TGE, then burn your PointsTokens to receive airdrop tokens. Airdrops may arrive in multiple tranches. You can redeem incrementally as each tranche is distributed.

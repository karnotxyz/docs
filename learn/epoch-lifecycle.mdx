---
title: "Epoch Lifecycle"
description: "Every strategy runs in fixed-duration epochs that progress through three states: Active, Settling, and Matured."
icon: "clock"
---

An **epoch** is a fixed time window during which a strategy vault operates. Each strategy defines its own epoch schedule. When one epoch ends, a new one can begin — capital does not automatically carry over.

Every epoch moves through exactly three states:

```mermaid
stateDiagram-v2
    [*] --> ACTIVE
    ACTIVE --> SETTLING : Epoch end reached
    SETTLING --> MATURED : Settlement complete
    MATURED --> [*]
```

**Active** — The strategy is executing and orderbooks are open. Users can deposit (minting ST and EPT), or trade ST and EPT on their respective orderbooks. Credits accrue on EPT balances throughout this phase.

**Settling** — The strategy unwinds its positions and capital is returned to the vault. No new deposits are accepted. Orderbooks remain open for secondary trades only — no new minting (Buy + Buy cross-matching is disabled). Credit accrual is frozen.

**Matured** — Settlement is complete. Trading stops. ST holders redeem for USDC at the final NAV. EPT holders claim PointsTokens based on their accumulated credit share. There is no deadline on redemption — tokens remain valid indefinitely. To participate in the next epoch, you redeem and deposit again.
